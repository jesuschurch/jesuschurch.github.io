God will not give his glory to another (Exodus 20:3-5, Isaiah 42:8, Isaiah 48:11). Behold, the Lord is a jealous God (Exodus 20:5). How, then, shall we expect to be guiltless upon praising women in the same way we praise God? Therefore I exhort thee to

  1. Recognize the idolatry of love songs, and reprove them. Ephesians 5:11
    And have no fellowship with unfruitful works of darkness, but rather reprove them.

    Indeed, reprove love songs for what they are, idolatry. They are unfruitful, for women are nothing; our help is of God (Psalms 60:12). And they are works of darkness, for praise is of the Lord alone, and the Lord our God is a jealous God (Exodus 20:5).

  2. Avoid love songs and although this is hard, for it seems as though nearly all songs are love songs nowadays, it is not good that thou listen to the hymns of the synagogue of women. Would thou, a Christian, feel comfortable listening to muslim prayers 24/7? Doth thou suppose this would harm thy relationship with the one true God? And although, yea, thou can listen to these prayers and not commit idolatry, for thou art only listening to them, thou should be wary that these prayers do not influence thee away from God. Same it is for love songs. If thou listen too much to them, thou too will become influenced by these, and thy thoughts will go unto women.

  3. Listen to Christian music, for it is the praise of God. Thus we shalt counter the worship of women of all the people of the earth.
