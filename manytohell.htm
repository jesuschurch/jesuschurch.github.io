  5. Then shall ye begin to say, We have eaten and drunk in thy presence, and thou hast taught in our streets.
  6. But he shall say, I tell you, I know you not whence ye are; depart from me, all ye workers of iniquity.
  7. There shall be weeping and gnashing of teeth, when ye shall see Abraham, and Isaac, and Jacob, and all the prophets, in the kingdom of God, and you yourselves thrust out.
