How to Cook Cereal

Welcome, dear friends, to the first article in my cooking series. Cooking is a great activity and is very fun. A well-versed cook has (nearly) complete control over what he eats and doesn't depend on others to cook for them. Cooking is rewarding; there is nothing like working hard on a meal, seeing it come out great, and eating it. Anyways, let us get on to the ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Cereal of your choosing that does not contain soy
  • Raw milk
  • A bowl
  • A spoon

Steps

  • Pour cereal unto the bowl. (Cereal must be first.)
  • Pour raw milk unto the bowl. (Raw milk must be last.)
  • Place the spoon unto the bowl.

Notes

Do not use a milk with soy. Soy products are strictly forbidden.
